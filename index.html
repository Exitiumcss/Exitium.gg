2D Radar

Draggable, resizable north-up radar with per-team dots, planted-bomb indicator, and outward arrows for off-range targets.

Distance Fade

True meter-based range filter with configurable fade curve. Far targets fade out gracefully instead of spamming your screen.

Animated Tracers

Gradient-faded snaplines from any origin — bottom, center, top, or crosshair — with off-screen direction arrows.

Damage Numbers

Floating "-N" numbers rise and fade when enemies take damage. Live HP tracking per steam ID, per frame.

Low-HP Pulse

Targets under your HP threshold get a pulsing red outline. You'll never miss the clean-up frag again.

faq

Answers, not asterisks.

Is exitium.gg really external?
Yes. It runs in its own process with a transparent overlay window, reads memory via standard user-mode APIs, and never injects, hooks, or writes to the game. No DLL, no driver.
Is it streamproof?
One toggle excludes the overlay from OBS, Discord, Windows Game Bar and default Windows capture. Your viewers see a clean game, you see the ESP.
What's the performance hit?
Designed for high-refresh. Optional VSync, smoothed FPS readout, and a streamlined render loop that batches every shape into a single ImGui draw list per frame.
How do I configure it?
Press INSERT or Right Shift to open the menu. Press END to save and exit. All settings persist to config.json.
Where can I get help?
Jump in the Discord — that's where updates, support, and community config presets live.
